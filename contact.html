Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common queries regarding account creation, screening rules, and security.

How does the developer verification matching system work?
Stackly analyzes candidate profiles (including work experience, GitHub portfolios, and skills benchmarks) against recruiter-specified configurations to flag immediate, highly matching profiles instead of relying on basic keyword searches.
Is the candidate portfolio and resume builder free to use?
Yes, the resume template builder and dashboard search interfaces are completely free for job seekers. Advanced analytics reports and personalized application tracking alerts are included.
How does Stackly verify recruiters and job postings?
All corporate domains, business numbers, and posted jobs go through manual validation reviews by our talent success team to ensure listings represent active hiring roles with verified compensation rates.
What is the application status timeline tracker?
The application tracker displays the real-time stage of your application (Applied, Screening, Interview, Offer, Hired). As recruiters move cards on their recruitment pipelines, candidate dashboards update automatically.
Can I post multiple job listings as a recruiter?
Yes. Standard recruiter accounts let you post and manage multiple active listings, organize candidates into pipeline boards, and coordinate candidate communication panels.
How is user session information secured?
Stackly stores session tokens and candidate database structures directly inside your browser's secure localStorage sandbox. No credentials, tokens, or profile data are transmitted to unverified external databases.
